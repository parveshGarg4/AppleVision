About Us

Learn More About Us

Welcome to our Capstone project website! We are a team of passionate engineers who have come together to help our country's apple cultivators with a portable and automated grading and sorting machine. Our mission is to make the apple grading and sorting process more efficient and effective, while also reducing the time and resources required.

Our machine uses a combination of advanced sensors and technologies to achieve this goal. By integrating different sensors, such as color sensors, weight sensors, and size sensors, our machine can quickly and accurately sort apples based on their quality and characteristics.

  • Focuses on helping our country's apple cultivators.
  • Provides a portable and automated grading and sorting machine.
  • Eliminates human error and ensures consistency in the sorting process.

Our portable design makes it easy for apple cultivators to move the machine to different locations and sort apples on-site, which saves time and reduces transportation costs. Plus, the automated grading and sorting process enables apple cultivators to increase their productivity and profitability.

Why Choose AppleVision?

Despite advances in agricultural sensing and automation technologies over the past decade, infield grading and sorting practices for commercial apple harvests have not been adopted due to a lack of appropriate technologies that meet cost, throughput, reliability, and robustness requirements. This situation is often exploited by large multinational companies, leaving farmers with no choice but to resort to them.
